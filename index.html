Who am I!?

With a bachelor's degree in fine arts, drawing has been apart of Nick's life like my right arm. After discovering UI/UX design incorporated all of his passions, Nick deciding to make a career out of it. Self-teaching frontend development and graphic design has led him to discover the UI/UX Design track at BLOC. While completing the course, Nick also obtained his NASM-CPT and became a personal trainer to supplement income. As much as he enjoys personal training, the UI and UX field is where his passions are. For Nick, there is a world of adventure being skipped over when not attempting to learn more UX or working on a project. UX consumes him like art has and Nick is eager to start his career.
