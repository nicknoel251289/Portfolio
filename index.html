Who is Nick!?

With a bachelor's degree in fine arts, drawing has been a part of of my life like my right arm has. After discovering that UI/UX design incorporated all of my passions, I deciding to make a career out of it. Self-teaching frontend development was like playing with Lego's to me. Upon playing with my virtual Legos, I discovered the UI/UX Design track at BLOC. While completing the course, I also obtained my NASM-CPT to become a personal trainer to supplement income. As much as I enjoy personal training, the UI and UX field is where my passions are. I get an apprehensive feeling of missing out when I am not trying further myself learning more about UX, crafting a drawing, or working on a project. UX consumes me like art has and I am truley eager to start my career.
